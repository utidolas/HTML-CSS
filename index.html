Naturave #1 - Into the woods

Monteverde, Provincia de Puntarenas, Costa Rica

Description

NATURAVE is a treetops unique stay experience. A one of a kind cabin where, from the comfort of your bedroom, you can enjoy the birds singing, sightseeing families of monkeys passing in the mountain just in front of you or just enjoy a cup of coffee surrounded by the peace of Monteverde. On the first floor you will find the entrance, a relaxation space surrounded by nature, a coffee station, a dining table and the bathroom, when climbing the spiral stairs, you will find a cozy room inside a dome made with local wood and just in front, a balcony with view to the mountains.
